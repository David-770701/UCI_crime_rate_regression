## Submission notes

One team member shall submit the project for the whole group. The submission consists of the following:

- Presentation Slides
- Programming code (Jupyter Notebook is not accepted, use .R, .py or .m)

## Project Scope

This is a team project. The recommended size for the team is 3 students (size of 4 is also allowed).

Your goal is to show your understanding and practical use of concepts and methodologies in regression analysis that we learned this semester. Your presentation aims to impress the audience (students and instructors) with communication and project implementation skills. You are encouraged to be innovative. However, to better evaluate your performance, the following required points must be implemented in your project and presented:

1. [Required] Presentation cover page with the name and student number of each team member.
2. [Required] Background, Motivation & Objective of your analysis
3. [Required] Introduction to the data: describe your data and variables in detail
4. [Recommended] Data exploratory: Exploratory plots, summary statistics
5. [Recommended] Data cleaning, transformations
6. [Required] Diagnostics checks & model transformations
7. [Required] Model selection, residual analysis
8. [Required] Regression model specification and estimation results interpretations
9. [Recommended] Testing and interpretations
10. [Recommended] Other analysis you did
11. [Required] Conclusion of your topic analysis

You are free to choose any dataset for your analysis. We will propose some during the semester (check iSpace). Also the following are useful data repositories:

a. https://www.kaggle.com/datasets
b. https://archive.ics.uci.edu
c. https://data.sh.gov.cn/view/data-resource/index.html
